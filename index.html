check_postgres.pl

check_postgres.pl is a script for checking the state of one or more Postgres databases and reporting back in a Nagios-friendly manner. It was developed by Greg Sabino Mullane of End Point Corporation and is BSD-licensed. The latest version is 1.7.1, and was released on June 2, 2008.
